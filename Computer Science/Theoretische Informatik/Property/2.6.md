The function $bin$ is [[Basic Notation and Terms#Functions|injective]] and $bin(2n+a) = bin(n)\cdot a$ for $n \geq 1$ and $a \in \{0,1\}$
## Proof


## Example
bin(54) = 110110

| Num | Multiplication | Rest<br> |
| --- | -------------- | -------- |
| 54  | 2 * 27         | 0        |
| 27  | 2 * 13         | 1        |
| 13  | 2 *  6         | 1        |
| 6   | 2 * 3          | 0        |
| 3   | 2 * 1          | 1        |
| 1   | 2 * 0          | 1        |
$$\Rightarrow bin(54) = 110110$$
